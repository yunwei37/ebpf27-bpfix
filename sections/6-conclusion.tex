\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

An eBPF program loads only after a static verifier proves it safe, and on rejection the verifier returns a message that does not say how to repair the program. Across \bench, a corpus of 235 reproduced rejections, this message under-determines the repair, leaving the bug, the responsible layer, and the fix site for the developer to recover. Reading only the log, \sys reconstructs the safety proof the verifier discarded and locates the fix site from the verifier's own per-instruction states. On \testset, a 75-case repair suite, the \sys diagnostic raises a downstream model's repair from 22 of 75 to 38 in one shot, and from 30 to 44 with one retry. Attributing the responsible layer remains best-effort, and a frozen heldout benchmark is future work.
